About the App

WorkoutEvaluator helps you log, track, and review your workouts — all in one place. Whether you're just getting started or training consistently, WorkoutTracker keeps your progress organized and easy to follow.

  • Log exercises, sets, reps, and weights
  • Track your workout history over time
  • Stay consistent and hit your goals
  • Recieve trend analysis and feedback

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a workout?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen to start a new workout session and add your exercises.

Is my data saved if I close the app?

Yes — your workout history is saved automatically as you go.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to evan.nelson.dev@gmail.com with a description of the issue and your device model. Screenshots are always helpful!
